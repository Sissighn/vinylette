`vinylette-mock-cover.png` is a fictional album cover created specifically for
the Vinylette portfolio screenshots with OpenAI image generation on 2026-07-19.
It is not copied from or based on a third-party album cover and is distributed
with this repository under the project MIT license.

Generation prompt:

> Create an entirely original square mock album cover artwork for the macOS app
> Vinylette and its public portfolio README. Art direction: refined mid-century
> modern meets Art Deco, abstract midnight garden with flowing botanical shapes,
> a small crescent moon and subtle constellation geometry; palette limited to
> deep burgundy, dusty rose, warm ivory, antique gold, and near-black. Premium
> editorial illustration, tactile screen-print texture, elegant balanced
> composition, strong center crop that also reads clearly inside a small
> circular vinyl label. No people, no recognizable characters, no existing
> album-art references, no brands, no logos, no typography, no letters, no
> watermark. The artwork must be original and copyright-safe as a fictional
> album cover. Output a flat front-facing square cover only, edge to edge, with
> no mockup, border, record, case, background scene, or perspective.
